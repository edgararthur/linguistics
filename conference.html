UpComing Conference

15th Annual Linguistics Association of Ghana Conference Exploring Language Diversity: Connecting Past, Present, and Future We are thrilled to invite you to the 15th Annual Linguistics Association of Ghana Conference, a platform that celebrates the rich tapestry of languages within Ghana and beyond. This year's conference is centered around the theme "Rethinking Languages and Linguistics. Research for Sustainable Development in the 21st century" where linguists, researchers, educators, and language enthusiasts come together to delve into the multifaceted aspects of languages, their evolution, and their role in shaping societies.

Event Details:
Date: Wed 30th August - Fri, 1st September
Venue: Takoradi Technical University, Takoradi, Ghana

Theme: Rethinking Languages and Linguistics. Research for Sustainable Development in the 21st century
